# FABRIC OVERVIEW – QUICK COMPARISON <span id=_Tocln3w0v0s0q68></span>  
## Cotton vs Canvas vs Jute vs JUCO vs Linen <span id=_Toclyrermfh21j6></span>  
This page helps buyers quickly compare fabrics and select the  **right material based on use, durability, look, and budget** .   
---  
  
|  Parameter <br>|  Cotton <br>|  Canvas <br>|  Jute <br>|  JUCO (Jute + Cotton) <br>|  Linen / Linen Blend <br>|
|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
|  **Fiber Type** <br>|  Natural cotton <br>|  Heavy cotton / cotton blend <br>|  Natural jute fiber <br>|  Jute blended with cotton <br>|  Flax fiber (linen), often blended <br>|
|  **Typical GSM** <br>|  140–220 GSM <br>|  270–540 GSM (8–16 oz) <br>|  250–400 GSM <br>|  280–360 GSM <br>|  180–240 GSM <br>|
|  **Durability** <br>|  Medium <br>|  **Very High** <br>|  High <br>|  High <br>|  Medium <br>|
|  **Load Capacity** <br>|  Light–Medium <br>|  **Heavy duty** <br>|  Medium–Heavy <br>|  Medium–Heavy <br>|  Light–Medium <br>|
|  **Look & Feel** <br>|  Soft, clean <br>|  Structured, premium <br>|  Rustic, natural <br>|  Smooth + natural <br>|  Elegant, premium <br>|
|  **Print Quality** <br>|  **Excellent** <br>|  Excellent <br>|  Moderate <br>|  **Very good** <br>|  Moderate <br>|
|  **Shape Retention** <br>|  Low–Medium <br>|  **Excellent** <br>|  Medium <br>|  Medium <br>|  Low <br>|
|  **Eco Profile** <br>|  Reusable, eco-friendly <br>|  Reusable, long life <br>|  100% biodegradable <br>|  Natural blend <br>|  Natural, low-impact <br>|
|  **Price Level** <br>|  Low–Medium <br>|  Medium–High <br>|  Medium <br>|  Medium–High <br>|  High <br>|
|  **Best For** <br>|  Promotions, retail <br>|  Retail, grocery <br>|  EU gifting, wine <br>|  Premium eco retail <br>|  Boutique, luxury <br>|
